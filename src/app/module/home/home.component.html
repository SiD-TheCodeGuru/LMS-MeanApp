From the CEO's Desk

Welcome to our Learning Management System (LMS) portal named as Tripura Election Training and Assessment Portal) which has been designed & developed with the objective of providing learning materials to the personnel engaged for various roles & responsibilities in the state Assembly & Parliamentary Elections.

This portal strives to be a one-stop platform for the online assessment of manpower to be deployed for conduction of the Elections in Tripura. It contains features for registration of the personnel to be deployed for the election duty. They will be trained by the Assembly Level Master Trainer and on completion of the training an assessment shall be carried out online through this portal. E-Certificate shall be awarded to the trainees completing the assessment satisfactorily.

The portal shall also provide facility for repeat test if required for better understanding of the rules & procedures to be adopted during the election process for successful and smooth conduction of the same.

I appeal to all the personnel engaged in the Election Duty under various responsibility positions to take advantage of this portal and undergo the assessment process to be carried through this portal for better understanding of the duty functions and improvement if necessary in order to be able to execute the Election duties in an efficient way for smooth conduction of the Elections for the state of Tripura.
